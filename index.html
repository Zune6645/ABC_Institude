Introduction

This is a website where we may take ABC Learning Institute's courses. ABC Learning Institute prides itself on providing clients with the essential and relevant training, as well as the resources they require. The courses and solutions are continually examined in order to deliver the most up-to-date and information knowledge to the clients.

This project was created in order to complete one of the BTEC tasks. We were given the task of creating a website that would allow visitors to browse the courses and their schedules. Pages such as the Home Page, About Us, Contact Us, Policy, and Sitemap should also be included.
